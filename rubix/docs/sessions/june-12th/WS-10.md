## Scope / deliverables

- A bounded outbound buffer per channel class with a declared overflow policy: `cur` = latest/
  drop-oldest, `write`/`his` = reliable (bounded queue, backpressure/retry, no silent drop â€” a full
  reliable queue surfaces an error, never discards).
- An ack path for `write` commands: the responder acknowledges application of a priority-array write;
  the sender retries within the bounded buffer until ack or a declared give-up that surfaces a spark/
  error (no infinite retry, no silent loss).
- A `log()`-visible counter of dropped `cur` samples under pressure (no silent truncation).
- Tests: under a flooded `cur` channel, oldest samples drop and newest survive; a `write` is retried
  and acked; a saturated reliable buffer surfaces an error rather than dropping.
